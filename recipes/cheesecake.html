Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a dessert consisting of a creamy cheese filling, often made with cream cheese or other soft cheeses, eggs, and sugar, atop a crust made from cookies, graham crackers, or pastry.

Ingredients

  • graham cracker crumbs
  • melted butter
  • sour cream
  • all-purpose flour
  • vanilla extract
  • cream cheese
  • white sugar
  • milk
  • eggs
  • lemon zest
  • orange zest

Steps

  1. Make the crust and press it into a prepared springform pan.
  2. Make the filling and pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake until the edges are puffed and the center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven.
