Hazelnut Frappe

Description

A hazelnut frappe is a blended iced coffee drink that combines the flavors of coffee and hazelnut, often with a creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop (30g) MONIN Chocolate
  • Frappé powder
  • 30ml MONIN Hazelnut syrup
  • 120ml milk
  • scoop of ice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blending jug and mix until fully combined.
  2. Pour into a milkshake glass, top with whipped cream and MONIN Dark Chocolate sauce
